An intelligent PC recommendation engine powered by fine-tuned DistilBERT, multi-agent orchestration via CrewAI, and deterministic hardware compatibility checking.

Classifies your needs, recommends the best build, validates every component, and requires your approval — a true human-in-the-loop pipeline.
BRIM
Groupe · École Supérieure d'Informatique et du Numérique
BuildMate is a multi-agent AI system that helps users find the most suitable PC build or laptop according to their needs, preferences, and budget. The system combines natural language understanding, deep learning, deterministic recommendation logic, and multi-agent orchestration to deliver explainable, validated hardware recommendations.
